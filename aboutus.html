About Us

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society is a non-profitable organisation at Paharpur in the district of Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. We started our journey in the year of 2016 and with God’s grace, we are still growing. Our Society is registered under section West Bengal Societies Registration Act, 1961. Our registered office is at Annagh Mohan Roy, s/o Gajendra Roy, Hakimpara, P.O. Jalpaiguri, WB. Pin – 735109.


From the very beginning we are trying to give a sustainable development to the people who are underprivileged and from deprived class. Our main working area is in 8 districts of JALPAIGURI, DARJEELING, COOCHBEHAR, NORTH AND SOUTH DINAJPUR AND ALIPURDUAR in West Bengal.

We focus about people that they should get aware of the govt schemes regarding health and other facilities. So, they can know what are their rights. We want that people of North Bengal who are living a destitute life for a long period of time should come round this phase. We are trying hard to increase the income of those people from 20% to 60% in one year(2018-2019 up to 2020. Our development team involved near about 40 villages of North Bengal.


We are trying to do a lots of works together. We have projects from income generation to health awareness, to creation care and so many social issues. For all these huge activities we need a lot of human efforts. Here we thank to all our volunteers (sahayaks) and all staffs and members for being with us in our journey.

Mission

The main motto of our society, Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society is to bring about a change in the target community whereby they have greater control over their lives and environment in areas of social, economical, Political and spiritual development.

Vision

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society wants to grow big and reach to every corner to help the neediest person. Our future goal is to provide a complete evolution in the lifestyle of the people of North Bengal.

Aims and Objectives

Our society wants to generate employment and provide jobs to the unemployed youths. Many opportunities and jobs are available in various private institutions and our duty is to establish the youths into that.


We inspire the religious feelings, spirit of harmony and tolerance. By developing all these we try to bond a brotherhood amongst the common people and to feel them as one true citizen of India.


We try to educate the children of the slum areas against the present environment pollution and to safeguard them from the provocation of street begging and child labor.


Our society encourage children to get educate. For this and for the benefit of the local people we establish an orphanage, library, free reading room, primary school, etc.


We help the deserving and talented students especially orphans boys or girls. We providing help by giving uniforms, books and other assistance from the fund of the Society.


We provide help to the local people who are poor, indigent, aged and sick or homeless. Along with these we also help our members of the Society in case of marriage, sickness, accident, and death or any natural calamities.


Our society always encourage to cultivate the spirit of social, cultural and humanitarian activities among the local people. We often bear ethical treatment to the animals who live in the streets.
