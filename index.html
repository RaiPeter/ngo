Our Mission

The main motto of our society, Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society work for spreading various awareness. People are practicing with right way to change their life by socially.

Our Vision

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society wants to grow big and reach to every corner to help the neediest person. Our future goal is to provide a complete evolution in the lifestyle of the people of North Bengal.

Our Objectives

Our society wants to generate employment and provide jobs to the unemployed youths. Many opportunities and jobs are available in various private institutions and our duty is to establish the youths into that.

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society is a non-profitable organisation at Paharpur in the district of Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. We started our journey in the year of 2016 and with God’s grace, we are still growing. Our Society is registered under section West Bengal Societies Registration Act, 1961. Our registered office is at Annagh Mohan Roy, s/o Gajendra Roy, Hakimpara, P.O. Jalpaiguri, WB. Pin – 735109.


From the very beginning we are trying to give a sustainable development to the people who are underprivileged and from deprived class. Our main working area is in 8 districts of JALPAIGURI, DARJEELING, COOCHBEHAR, NORTH AND SOUTH DINAJPUR AND ALIPURDUAR in West Bengal. Read More

Our Work

Income Generation

For the benefits of the villagers and IGAs ( Income Generating Activities), our society support generate this income generation program. We try to initiate these programs located near the homes of the beneficiaries. We encourage man/ woman to use their skills which they already possess.

Health Awareness

Nowadays, one of the biggest challenges that North Bengal facing is Rural Healthcare. In these areas poor health condition is caused for high mortality rates. More than 60% of the main population of North Bengal is living under the roof of rural setup.

Creation Care

Paharpur Ujjala Samaj Human Care Welfare Society cares for creation. Our mother earth has created so many wonders and this is our duty to protect them. Keeping this in mind we planted 3500 trees in different places of North Bengal.

Govt. Schemes & RTI

We organise every year to educate the people about Govt. scemes. There are many schemes for the rural people and also for widows, physically challenged or for children and pregnant women implemented by the Govt. But in many cases we often see that they are not getting those facilities

Social Issues

Often adolescents girls are the victms here. In rural and remote areas of India, many people are suffering from this issue. The humans are forced to slavery, lobour or sexual exploitation. We make the people aware and teach them to stop this and save our girls.
